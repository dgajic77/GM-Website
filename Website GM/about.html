About Us

Gajic Motorsport is a competitive racing team focused on hill climbs and circuit racing. Our team combines engineering precision with driver talent to push every machine to its absolute limit.

Built from pure passion for motorsport, we dedicate ourselves to constant improvement. On the track and behind the scenes. From setup testing to custom vehicle development, our goal is simple: **be the fastest.**
